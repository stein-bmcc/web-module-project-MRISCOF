Cochabamba

Sharing the name with its own capital

Cochabamba is one of the most important cities in Bolivia

This city is almost as important as Santa Cruz de la Sierra, thanks of its industrial and commerce importance. This city's most interesting attraction is the statue of "El Cristo de la Concordia" being a statue of Jesus Christ, taller than "Cristo Redentor" in Brazil.
