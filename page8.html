Oruro

Not far from La Paz, Oruro has one of the best Carnivals in the world

Oruro city (the capital of the department) can be considered as an industrial city, but the city has more than just industries and mines. In the city you can find one of the biggest Carnival parades in the world. Being the Carnival of Oruro an Intagible Cultural Heritage by the UNESCO.
