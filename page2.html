La Paz

Oficially called "Nuestra Senora de La Paz"

The capital of the Department "La Ciudad de La Paz" is the metropoli of Bolivia

As the rest of the country it is rich in culture

What makes La Paz special is that the City is built over around 3600 meters (11,975) above the sea, almost half of the Mount Everest. La Paz is proud of having the "Illimani" a tall mountain, that can be seeing from every part of the city, with its charasteristic three peaks. La paz has the ruins of one of the oldest civilizations in South America and the world, Tiwanaku. It also shares the Titicaca Lake, the highest navigable lake in the world. In La Paz, you can be in the Mountain Range of the Andes and travel for a couple of hours and be in the Amazon Forest, and still be in the same department.
