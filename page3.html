Santa Cruz

The main city and capital is called "Santa Cruz de la Sierra"

considered to be the next metropoli of Bolivia in the following years

This part of the country is part of the Amazon Forest, Santa Cruz is very rich in animal life and popular for its big forest and rivers, being the main exporter of meat and other natural goods. Santa Cruz is becoming the wealthiest department in Bolivia, not mentioning the warm and calm weather, you can enjoy in a daily basis.
