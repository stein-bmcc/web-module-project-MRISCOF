Pando

Pando the hidden gem.

Pando also known as the forgoten department by some people in Bolivia, is the rainiest department in Bolivia, it bases its economony mainly in agriculture. But it has one of the best parts to navigate the Amazon river, having usual sights of pink dolphins and calm waters to enjoy the view of the forest.
