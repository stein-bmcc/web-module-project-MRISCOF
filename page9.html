Beni

Beni is a more agrarian department, and rich in nature.

This whole section of the country is incredibly rich in nature. Being completely covered by the Amazon Forest, the daily sight of animals in everyone's life is normal. You can easily spot from all different kinds of lizards to even monkeys if you go a little bit deep into the forest, and if you are lucky, maybe a pink dolphin in a river.
