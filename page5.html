Tarija

Tarija city being the capital is also known as the capital of the smile by the rest of the country

Also the capital of wine

Tarija's main income is the wine, and imports from Argentina. Many people from different parts of the world go to Tarija exclusively for the wine. It also has its own Carnival, but differently from other parts of the country it also has its own costumes, traditions and other festivities attached to the wine consumption.
