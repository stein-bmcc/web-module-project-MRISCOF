Potosi

It holds the biggest treasure of Bolivia: El Salar de Uyuni (the Uyuni Salt Salt Flat)

Potosi, the city that used to be hold one of the biggest silver mines of the world, "El Cerro Rico de Potosi" which, used to be the crowdest city in the world during the Spanish colonization, has now the pride of most of the Bolivians thanks to the Uyuni Salt Flat. The largest Salt Flat of the world.
