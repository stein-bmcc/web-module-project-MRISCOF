Chuquisaca

Chuquisaca is the official capital of Bolivia, but a lot of people recognize Chuquisaca's capital as the real capital of the country, Sucre.

The main city "Sucre" is also known as the city of the four names, because of the different names that it adopted with the pass of the time. The four names being: Sucre, Chuquisaca, La Plata, and Charcas. An unofficial name is also "La Ciudad Blanca"
